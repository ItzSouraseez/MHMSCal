Pre-Primary



With over 18 years of experience, Mansur Habbibullah Memorial School has set a high standard in child development and education. The Pre-Primary section comprise classes of Pre-Nursery, Nursery, L.K.G. and U.K.G. with age-group ranging from 2.5 years to 5+ years.
Knowing that no two children are alike, the school provides for training where the different needs of the children are dealt with individually.

The Pre-Nursery class follows a play-way method of teaching which is inter-woven with the latest and modern teaching aids and apparatus.

The Nursery, L.K.G. and U.K.G. classes are under the vigilance of a team of highly qualified and experienced professionals who look after the children with care and love to ensure healthy development of the children from a very young age. Emphasis is given on teaching the children proper presentation skills and general manners.
